2.22.2  ATTENDANCE ELIGIBILITY

 

This form is used to specify students who are eligible & not eligible for exams.

 

Ø  Batch:  Select a batch.

Ø  Semester:  Select a semester.

Ø  Min%:  Give minimum attendance percentage.

 

NOTE:  Grid view includes details of Student code, Student Name, Count of Subjects and Eligibility. In grid count of subjects which are equal to and less than min% are displayed. Uncheck students who are not eligible for exams and update the grid view.
